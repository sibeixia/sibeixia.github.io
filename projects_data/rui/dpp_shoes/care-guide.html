Care Instructions

Gently wipe the shoes with a soft, dry cloth after wear to remove surface dust
For suede leather areas, use a soft suede brush to clean in one direction and restore texture
For knitted polyester panels, lightly brush or wipe with a dry cloth; avoid excessive friction
Do not machine wash or soak in water
If the shoes become wet, allow them to air dry naturally at room temperature
Avoid direct heat sources, prolonged moisture, or high humidity
Do not use bleach, solvents, or harsh chemicals on any part of the shoe
Store the shoes in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
Use shoe trees when not in use to help maintain shape and reduce creasing
