Carbon Footprint

Water Footprint

Estimated
~1,800 L / watch

Environmental Standards & Compliance

Compliant with RoHS (Restriction of Hazardous Substances) requirements

Manufacturing facilities operate under ISO 14001:2015 environmental management systems

Designed to align with the European Union Digital Product Passport (DPP) regulatory framework, supporting transparency, traceability, and environmental information disclosure across the product lifecycle

Energy Usage

Renewable energy sources are partially used during manufacturing processes.

Sustainability Philosophy

HERITAGE LUXURY WATCH emphasizes durability, repairability, and long product lifecycles to minimize environmental impact while preserving traditional watchmaking craftsmanship.
