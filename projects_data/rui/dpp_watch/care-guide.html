Care Guide

Care Instructions

  • Clean the case and leather strap regularly with a soft, dry cloth
  • Avoid contact with water, chemicals, perfumes, and cleaning agents
  • Avoid sudden or extreme temperature changes
  • Protect the watch from strong shocks and impacts
  • Avoid prolonged exposure to strong magnetic fields
  • Store the watch in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight when not in use
  • Have the watch professionally serviced every 3–5 years
  • Periodically check water resistance if the model is water-resistant
